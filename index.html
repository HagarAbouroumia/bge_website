Who we are?

BUILDERS GROUP EGYPT is an established construction company that holds the power of integration in construction and development. We serve industrial, commercial, institutional and residential projects. BGE is committed to generating personalized, innovative, quality-driven solutions that fulfill client expectations and deliver superior outcome. At BGE, we implement visions to build nations. We provide turnkey services from the initial stage of designing through building, site installation prior to delivering. BGE possesses multiple construction and building divisions thus provides integrated solutions to mega projects in one place which serves high quality and avoids the hassle of multiple contractors. We provide services that involve CONCRETE, INFRASTRUCTURE, MEP, MARBLE, STEEL & ALUMINUM, LANDSCAPE and full fit in solutions.
